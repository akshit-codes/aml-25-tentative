Auto Expo

The Auto Expo at Amalthea 2025 is IIT Gandhinagar's premier automotive showcase on 8th & 9th November 2025. This major event brings together leading automobile brands, innovators, and enthusiasts with a dynamic platform of engaging showcases and competitions. Witness 80+ years of automotive evolution, featuring rare vintage treasures (like the 1943 Willys MB WWII Jeep) alongside cutting-edge showroom displays from Mercedes-Benz, Toyota, BYD, and Volkswagen. It stands as Gujarat's largest student-led celebration of automotive excellence.

  • Venue: Near Rangmanch and U-corridor
  • Time: 10:00AM onwards
