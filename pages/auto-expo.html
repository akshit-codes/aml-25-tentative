Auto Expo 2025

The Auto Expo at Amalthea 2025, IIT Gandhinagar's premier automotive showcase, is set to take place on 8th & 9th November 2025 as part of the institute's annual technical summit. Celebrating the theme “Orchestrating Origins,” the event brings together leading automobile brands, innovators, and enthusiasts to celebrate the spirit of engineering and mobility. From luxury and sports cars to electric and vintage vehicles, the Auto Expo offers a dynamic platform to experience automotive excellence and the technology shaping the future of transportation.

Building on the massive success of previous editions that featured brands like BMW, Triumph, and Harley Davidson, this year's expo promises an even grander experience with high-voltage events such as the Rev Battle, Modified Car Showcase, and Electric Mobility Zone. With an expected footfall of 5,000+ visitors and participation from major automotive names, the Auto Expo at Amalthea 2025 stands as Gujarat's largest student-led celebration of automotive innovation and design.

  • Venue: IIT Gandhinagar
  • Dates: 8th & 9th November 2025
