Shunya

The wait is over: Amalthea '25 proudly presents SHUNYA, its first-ever Electronic Dance Music (EDM) concert. Headlining the event is the globally acclaimed artist, TERI MIKO, who has previously headlined Sunburn—Asia's largest music festival—and become the first Indian artist signed to Tchami's prestigious Confession Records. Endorsed by industry legends including Tiësto, Martin Garrix, and Diplo, Teri Miko is set to deliver an electrifying night of pure, high-energy sound on November 8th. The event is free for the IIT Gandhinagar community, with passes available for external guests.

  • Venue: Rangmanch IITGN
  • Time: 7:30PM onwards
