AMALTHEA 2025

Who are we?

Amalthea is India's first student-run Annual Technical Summit, proudly organized by the passionate students of IIT Gandhinagar since 2010. It's not just another tech fest; it's a vibrant, energetic space where innovation, creativity, and technology come together, powered by young minds who love to push the boundaries. Every year, Amalthea brings together students, industry leaders, researchers, and academics to connect, collaborate, and share ideas. From exciting hands-on workshops and challenging competitions to inspiring talks and a buzzing tech expo, we offer a variety of experiences designed to ignite curiosity and spark innovation.
Our goal is to create a platform where fresh ideas meet real-world challenges, encouraging everyone to think critically and come up with bold, practical solutions. But it's not all serious; Amalthea is also about having fun, making connections, and building a community that celebrates learning and creativity in a lively, offline setting. Whether you're a tech enthusiast, a curious learner, or someone looking to network with the best and brightest, Amalthea is your stage to discover, innovate, and make an impact. Join us as we go beyond the impossible and shape the future together.
